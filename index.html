研究目標 | Our overarching goal is to:

建立一個有系統且嚴謹的設計方法以提供不確定因素下的最佳化、穩健、且可靠的設計方針。
“Provide Systematic and Rigorous Approaches for Optimal, Robust, and Reliable Design under Uncertainty”
